Access to the Campus

Direction from Chofu Station

The UEC campus is a short walk from Chofu Station (Keio Line).

  • Take the Keio Line from Shinjuku Station (approx. 15-20 min by Special Express/Express).
  • Get off at Chofu Station and take the Central Exit.
  • Walk north for about 10 minutes to reach the main gate of the University of Electro-Communications.
